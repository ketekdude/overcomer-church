Get to know our pastor

Ps. Berry Pratna

Senior Pastor of Overcomer Church

In his desire to pursue God’s holy calling for this generation, Ps. Berry Pratna found Overcomer Church in October 2008. Overcomer Church’s vision is to build future leaders that overcomes the challenges of life and crosses all limitations through faith in the Word of God. The church adopted the loyalty and disloyalty teaching from Bishop Dag Heward-Mills at the heart of its volunteers.
